# 2025

In 2025, we hosted Bergen Open Source for the third year in a row, and the conference was a huge success! A big thank you to everyone who attended. A lot of exciting knowledge was shared, and we hope it sparked interest and motivation to dive further into the world of open source 🔍

There was a great atmosphere throughout the event, and we are proud to be part of such an engaged, active, and inclusive community as open source is.

We would also like to extend a big thank you to the speakers who took the time to share their knowledge and experiences, and to our dedicated friByte members for their efforts. This conference would not have been possible without you 🙌

We are already looking forward to seeing you next year 🚀

Here you can see the program for the conference, as well as slides and video.

- **Time:** October 30th 2025, 16:30-21:30
- **Location:** Storelogen @ Kvarteret, [Olav Kyrres gate 49, Bergen](https://www.openstreetmap.org/node/6137059358)
